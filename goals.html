My Goals as a Student

Maintain an "A" in every class

Instead of focusing solely on the final GPA, concentrate on earning an "A" on every assignment, test, and project throughout the semester.

Master active learning techniques

Go beyond passive reading and embrace proven study strategies to retain and apply information more effectively.

Prioritize physical and mental health

Sustainable academic performance requires taking care of your body and mind. High-achieving students recognize that neglecting their health leads to burnout and affects their grades

Practice tests

Work through practice questions or past exams to familiarize yourself with the format and types of questions. For technical courses like math, practice problems are more important than just re-reading the text
